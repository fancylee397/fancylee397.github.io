DEMAGE built shiny booth to assist JINLI show on 2015 nanjing telecom expo winning great popularity

On July the 3-5th, the 2015 Tianyi (E-Surfing) Terminal Expo & Summit Forum ( also known as the Nanjing Telecom Expo) was held in Nanjing expo center, as the leading enterprise in the china telecommunication industry, JINLI telecom cooperated with DEMAGE MESSE http://www.demage.com/english/ mighty present on this expo. JINLI wined great popularity with its charming booth and the newest high-tech mobile products exhibited on it, thus became the determined spotlight in this expo.

Based on the world 1st booth EI system, concerned on the standard KingLi colors from many enterprise identity factors, the Demage designers blended them fully into the booth design with the great and steady white as the basics cohered with warm orange red, which accurately met with the KingLi enterprise spirits on continuous innovations and the exploration will into future, also it metaphorized the enterprise as the rising sun, to continuously play its important role in future telecommunication domain.

After deep investigation into KingLi mainstream products - the m5 phone functions and feathers, demage experts intimately setup the “super duration” labeled lighting box in the entrance area, in the purpose to attract the audiences at the first time, to interest them to enter the booth for visiting, additionally, a special exhibition area was clearly emptied up to directly show the M5 phone strong duration and high profiles to audiences.

In order to offer better services for audiences, also to embody the enterprise humanized service ideas, demage setup free drinking bar and the charging station for just-in-time phone charging, such intimate arrangements and hardware configuration wined general praises from audiences, this driver the brand image into deeply their minds and hearts.

During the exhibiting, the crowds flows quickly in front of the kingli booth, many exhibitors and individuals visitors enter the booth for visiting and inquiring, neither the delicate booth constructions nor the creative technology new products disappoint the visitors, togetherly contribute to help JINLI develop its domestic market brand reputation. this again help prove KingLi’s specialities and brand power that valued the clients trust.
